# 🤖 Meet Coach Max — your MEX process buddy

## TL;DR

Coach Max is an AI bot that lives in `#mex-sos-escalations`. When you have a question about an MEX process — refunds, Feather, shop, scholarships, induction kits, anything — just ask it in the channel and it'll reply in a thread with the answer, citing the SOP it came from.

Think of it as a friend who's read every SOP in the handbook and can find the answer in seconds.

**You don't need to learn anything special. Just ask in plain English.**

---

## 📍 Where does Coach Max live?

In the `#mex-sos-escalations` Slack channel.

Coach Max checks the channel every 5 minutes for new messages. When you post a question, you'll usually have an answer within 5 minutes.

---

## 💬 How to ask Coach Max a question

### The simple version: just post

**STEP 1 — You post a question** ✋
In `#mex-sos-escalations`, write your question normally. Don't worry about formatting or wording — just describe what you need.

⬇️

**STEP 2 — Coach Max sees it** 👀
The bot checks for new messages every 5 minutes.

⬇️

**STEP 3 — Coach Max replies in a thread** 💬
You get an answer that quotes the relevant SOP, with a friendly greeting using your name.

⬇️

**STEP 4 — You take action** ✅
If the answer's right, you're done. If something's off, see "What if Coach Max gets it wrong?" below.

### Example

> ***You (in the channel):***
> *Hey — a member is asking about getting an A&E refund. They paid in September but couldn't take the test. What's the process?*

> ***Coach Max (replying in a thread):***
> *Hey [your name]! Per our Refund Exception SOP, A&E reimbursements have a specific window for Fall 2024 enrollees who completed prerequisites but didn't take the A&E. If they're outside that window, this needs SOS approval. Here's the step-by-step...*

## ❓ Some things to know

### What if Coach Max gets it wrong?

Two simple options:

**Option 1 — React on the bot's answer:**
- ✅ **Green check** = "this answer was correct"
- ❌ **Red X** = "this answer was wrong"

Your reactions get logged so we can spot patterns and fix gaps.

**Option 2 — Reply in the thread with the right answer.**
Just respond in the thread with what the answer should have been. This helps the team learn (and if you're a MEX lead, it can update Coach Max's playbook directly — see the *"Coach Max KB update workflow"* canvas).

### What if Coach Max doesn't know?

If your question isn't in the SOPs, Coach Max will say so honestly — usually with: *"I don't have this in my SOP."* It'll also suggest who you should ask instead, based on our escalation chain.

It will **never** invent a process or make up an answer.

### Can I ask follow-up questions in the same thread?

Yes! Just reply in the thread and Coach Max will see your follow-up on the next 5-min tick and answer back.

### How fast is Coach Max?

Up to 5 minutes between when you ask and when it replies (it wakes up every 5 minutes to check for new messages).

### Does Coach Max see DMs?

No, it only watches `#mex-sos-escalations`. If you DM it, nothing will happen.

## ❓ Quick FAQ

**Do I have to tag Coach Max for it to see my message?**
Not required — just posting in the channel usually works. But if you want to make absolutely sure Coach Max picks it up, **@-mention it in your message** and it'll always respond.

**What if Coach Max doesn't reply to my message?**
Sometimes Coach Max skips messages that look more like a comment or status update than a question. No big deal — just **@-mention Coach Max** and re-post (or reply in the same thread). That guarantees it'll see it and answer.

**Can I use Coach Max for non-MEX questions?**
It's tuned for MEX process. For other domains, you'll get *"I don't have this in my SOP."*

**Can I see what SOP Coach Max used for its answer?**
Yes — every answer cites the source. Look for something like *"Per our Refund Exception SOP..."* or *"This is from Kimberly's LOR SOP..."*.

**Is everything Coach Max says accurate?**
It uses the SOPs we have in the knowledge base, but those SOPs can sometimes be out of date or incomplete. **That's what the ✅/❌ reactions are for** — your feedback helps us spot gaps. If something feels wrong, flag it.

**Who built it / who maintains it?**
Built by Angelica Villalobos with the MEX team. Going forward, **the team maintains it directly** — the MEX leads can update Coach Max's playbooks straight from Slack using the *"Coach Max KB update workflow"* canvas. No engineering ticket required.
