## Process: Adding A&E After Enrollment Due to Technical Issue

**When this comes up:** A member enrolled but could not add the A&E (Advanced & Executive) courses during enrollment because the system froze, errored out, or timed out. They want to add it now at the enrollment price.

**What to do:** This qualifies for a manual charge via Authorize.net. Follow these steps:

**Step 1 — Confirm consent and price**
- Confirm the member still wants to add A&E and agrees to the charge (written consent required before charging)
- Confirm the amount — typically $190 for A&E at enrollment price (verify current pricing)

**Step 2 — Charge via Authorize.net**
1. Log into Authorize.net
2. Tools → Charge a Credit Card
3. Transaction Type: **Authorize & Capture**
4. Enter card number, expiration (MMYY), CVV, and amount
5. Read back all details to the member before submitting
6. Order Description: "A&E Add-On — enrollment technical issue"
7. Enter member name and email → Submit

**Step 3 — Enroll in Feather**
- Open the member's Feather profile
- Manually enroll them into the A&E Certification and/or Credit Recommended course
- Go to Transactions → Add Transaction → select the A&E package → Payment Method: **Authorize (manual)** → Add Package Transaction

**Step 4 — Send receipt**
- Find the transaction in Authorize.net → Print → Save as PDF
- Email the PDF receipt to the member with a confirmation note

**Escalation:** If unsure about current A&E pricing or enrollment setup, check with SOS before charging.

## Process: Enrollment Graduation Set — Early Shipping

**When this comes up:** A member purchased the Enrollment Graduation Set during registration but has not yet completed induction, and requests their items be shipped before completing all steps.

**Context:** Graduation Sets are normally shipped after all induction steps are completed. However, early shipping is permitted — particularly for members with upcoming ceremonies or late-season enrollees. **SOS approval is not required** for early fulfillment. Encourage completing steps first when possible, but fulfill the request if the member asks directly or it's an escalation.

**What the Enrollment Graduation Set includes:** Stole, cords, medallion, and t-shirt (varies by chapter; some chapters like SNHU have a customized package). Often offered at a reduced cost during enrollment. The medallion may be personalized (engraved) — this affects processing cutoff times.

**Key cutoff times (Mon–Fri only):**
- Standard/ground orders: **12:00 PM EST** for same-day processing
- Personalized items (engraved medallion): **9:30 AM EST** for same-day processing
- Carrier pickup typically: **3:00–3:30 PM EST**

---

**Step 1 — Confirm shipping preference and charge if applicable:**
1. Ask if the member is okay with **standard ground shipping** (7–10 business days, no extra charge)
2. If they need it faster, confirm they want **expedited shipping** and which method:
   - 3–5 day shipping
   - 2-day rush shipping
3. To determine the shipping cost: go to the NSLS Shop checkout → enter the member's shipping address → get the estimated cost → **do not complete the purchase**, just note the amount
4. If expedited: charge the shipping fee through **Authorize.net**
5. Set expectations: NSLS is not responsible for guaranteeing delivery by a specific date — the carrier is. Confirm the member's graduation date and clarify that expedited shipping reduces the window but doesn't guarantee it.

**Step 2 — Sync the order using the Grad Set Dashboard:**
1. Sync the Graduation Set order using the **Grad Set Dashboard** in Feather
2. Double-check the member's mailing address for accuracy before syncing
3. If the set includes a personalized item (engraved medallion): ensure the sync is submitted before **9:30 AM EST** for same-day processing

**Step 3 — Add notes in Shopify:**
1. Locate the **Shop order** in Shopify (comment on the order page, not the customer profile page)
2. Tag **@Rebecca Hebert** and **@Shop Team** in the comment
3. Include in the comment (no greetings — be concise):
   - Shipping method selected
   - Amount charged
   - **Do NOT include a "needs it by" date or delivery deadline.** Shop has no control over carrier delivery timing and these requests are unhelpful/frustrating for the Shop Team.

   *Example:* `@Rebecca Hebert @Shop Team — 3-5 day shipping, paid $15.75.`

---

**Shipping options summary:**

| Method | Timeline | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Standard ground | 7–10 business days | Free; no extra charge |
| 3–5 day | 3–5 business days | Charge via Authorize; cost varies by location |
| 2-day rush | 2 business days | Charge via Authorize; cost varies by location |

**Common questions:**

*Can a pre-inducted member get early shipping?*
- Yes. Members who have not completed induction steps may request early shipping. Standard ground is free. Expedited shipping is optional and requires an Authorize charge.

*What if a personalized order is submitted after 9:30 AM EST?*
- It processes and ships the next business day, even with expedited shipping selected.

*What if a standard (non-personalized) order is submitted after 12:00 PM EST?*
- It ships the next business day.

*Do ground shipments have guaranteed delivery dates?*
- No. Ground shipping typically arrives in 7–10 business days but has no guaranteed date.

*Is SOS approval needed before processing early shipping?*
- No — early shipping for Enrollment Graduation Sets does not require SOS approval.

*How do I get the shipping cost before charging?*
- Go to the Shop checkout, add the member's address, and note the estimated cost. Do not complete the purchase — use Authorize to charge.
