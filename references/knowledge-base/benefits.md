## Process: Digital Badge Upload on Social Media

**Tags:** digital badge, NSLS badge, badge LinkedIn, badge Instagram, badge Facebook, badge Twitter, share badge, post badge, Credly badge, member badge, badge upload, where is my badge, can't find my badge

**When this comes up:** A member asks how to find or share their NSLS digital badge on LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, or other social platforms.

**Eligibility:** Digital badges are available to **fully inducted members** only. Pre-inducted members cannot access the digital badge yet.

**Steps:**

1. **Log in** to the member's area at nsls.org

2. **Navigate to Credentials Hub** — located in the left-hand menu of the Members Area

3. **Locate earned badges** — the Credentials Hub will display all digital badges the member has earned based on their program completions

4. **View badge details** — click on the specific badge to open its detail view; this page shows badge criteria and sharing options

5. **Share badge** — click the **Share** or social sharing icon/button within the badge detail view

6. **Copy the Public Link** — members can copy a direct link to their badge to share anywhere

7. **Share by platform:**
   - **LinkedIn:** Use the "Add to LinkedIn Profile" button if available, or manually add the badge as a Certification/Award on their LinkedIn profile using the public badge link
   - **Facebook / Twitter:** Copy the public badge link or download the badge image and post it with a caption
   - **Other platforms:** Download the badge image or use the public link

**LinkedIn — adding as a Certificate:**
1. Go to LinkedIn profile → click "Add profile section" → select "Licenses & Certifications"
2. Enter certificate name, issuing organization (National Society of Leadership and Success), and use the public badge URL as the credential URL

**Escalation:** Route to MEX reviewer if the member is inducted but cannot see their badge in the Credentials Hub.

## Process: Letter of Recommendation (LOR) Request

**Tags:** LOR, letter of recommendation, recommendation letter, member needs letter, NSLS recommendation, request a recommendation, scholarship recommendation, job recommendation, Nancy Castillo LOR

**When this comes up:** A member requests an LOR, asks about LOR eligibility, needs help downloading one, or needs NSLS to submit an LOR directly to a third party on their behalf.

**Eligibility:** Member must be **fully inducted**. Non-inducted members cannot access LORs.

**LOR types available:**
- General Use
- For Job
- Graduate School
- Advanced/Executive *(only if member completed both Advanced AND Executive certificates)*

**Delivery options:**
- **Download Now (Free)** — PDF delivered immediately
- **Premium Mail ($6.00)** — Hand-signed, embossed original copy mailed via first-class USPS to the address the member provides

**Snippet:** `LOR -Letter Only`

---

**Step 1 — Phone or email inquiry:**
1. Confirm the member is inducted — if not, they cannot access LORs
2. Direct them to log into nsls.org → **Credentials Hub** → **Request Letter of Recommendation**
3. Remind them: inducted members have **unlimited** LORs they can request

**Step 2 — Downloading the LOR from Credentials Hub:**
1. Log in at nsls.org → go to **Credentials Hub** under Members Area
2. Click **Request Letter of Recommendation**
3. Choose the letter type (General, For Job, Graduate School, or Advanced/Executive)
4. Fill in letter details: name, address, city, state, ZIP
5. Choose delivery method: Download Now or Premium Mail ($6.00)

**Step 3 — If MEX is submitting to a third party on the member's behalf:**
1. Have the member complete Step 2 and download the PDF first
   - Double-check they selected the correct letter type before downloading
2. Member emails the PDF to **support@nsls.org** with:
   - Subject line: `ATTN: [Your Name] – LOR Submission`
   - Any upload instructions from the requesting organization
   - List of all school(s)/employer(s)/organizations the letter is intended for
3. MEX Specialist uploads or sends the LOR per the institution's instructions
4. ⚠️ If the submission form requires personal character questions (personality traits, how long we've known the member, etc.) that we cannot skip or enter N/A for — **we cannot complete the request.** Use snippet `LOR -Letter Only` and explain:
   > "While we're happy to help upload the LOR, we may not be able to fulfill the request if the form requires us to answer personal questions about traits or character. If we cannot skip or enter N/A for those fields, we may need to decline. We recommend working with an advisor, mentor, or supervisor who can speak to your personal experiences."
5. Once submitted, send the member a confirmation email or screenshot of the successful upload

**Step 4 — Special Cases (approved by KC/MEL only, usually a CS request):**

If a school insists on contacting NSLS directly for the member's LOR:
- The admissions office may reach out to NSLS via:
  - Email: support@nsls.org
  - Phone: (201) 222-6544 or Toll-Free (800) 601-6248
  - Live Chat or Text: (551) 249-0282 (Monday–Friday, 9 a.m. – 7 p.m. ET)
- If the student's contact information is provided, a MEX Specialist will reach out to:
  - Confirm the member's written permission for NSLS to provide the letter to their school
  - Walk them through what details are needed to create or finalize the letter
- Once permission and details are received, the Specialist prepares the letter and sends it to the admissions office on the member's behalf
- ⚠️ This is an **exception process** — not standard SOP. It requires MEL approval.

**Step 5 — Post-submission in HubSpot:**
1. Log a note or pin the email sent to the third party
2. Update the subject or internal note: `LOR Submission Request – [Member Name]`
3. Set Category: **Member Benefits → LOR → MEX Submitting on Behalf**
4. If multiple institutions: create a HubSpot task per submission; mark complete after each
5. If confirmation received from the institution, forward it to the member's ticket
6. If no confirmation within 2–3 business days, follow up based on urgency

**Common questions:**

*Does the member get unlimited LORs?*
- Yes — inducted members can request as many as they need.

*What's the difference between Download Now and Premium Mail?*
- Download Now is free and instant (PDF). Premium Mail is $6.00 and delivers a hand-signed, embossed original by USPS.

*Can NSLS submit to multiple institutions at once?*
- Yes. Have the member list all destinations in their email to support@nsls.org. Create a HubSpot task for each to track submissions.

*What if the institution's form asks character questions NSLS can't answer?*
- Use snippet `LOR -Letter Only` — NSLS cannot answer personal character questions on behalf of members.
