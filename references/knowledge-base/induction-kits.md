## Process: International Kits for Online Chapters

**When this comes up:** A member does not have a US address when enrolling, or needs their induction kit shipped internationally after completing steps.

**Key rule:** The enrollment system does not accept international addresses. Members must use a US address (school or NSLS corporate) during enrollment, then provide their real international address to their chapter advisor for kit shipment.

**Step 1 — Enrollment address:**
- Ask if the member has a US billing address
- If **no US address**: instruct them to enter **"INTERNATIONAL"** in the first address box, then add the school's/NSLS corporate address in the second address box
  - **NSLS corporate address:**
    ```
    INTERNATIONAL
    4300 Biscayne Blvd. Suite 203
    Miami, Florida 33137
    ```
  - **Walden and SNHU members:** must use their school's mailing address specifically
  - **All others:** may use the NSLS corporate address
- Use the appropriate snippet for written communication: `International SNHU`, `International Walden`, `International Northcentral`, or `International Online`

**Step 2 — Kit shipping:**
- Inform the member they must provide their **real international mailing address** to their chapter advisor once they complete enrollment
- The advisor will forward the address to ensure the kit ships to the correct location once steps are complete

**Step 3 — Close the interaction:**
- Congratulate the member on their nomination and encourage them to reach out with any further questions

**Common questions:**

*Member doesn't know their school's address?*
- Open Feather CRM → search for the chapter → open the chapter page → find the address listed next to "Chapter Address"

*Member entered the school address but didn't receive their kit?*
- Most likely cause: "INTERNATIONAL" was not included in address box 1
- Update the address so "INTERNATIONAL" is in address box 1, then email **Danielle Roth** to ensure the kit is sent

*Who does the member contact with their real international address?*
- Their chapter advisor, listed under the welcome message in their member's area

**Template response (for written communication):**
> Hello,
>
> Thank you for your email. I apologize — our application is not currently set up to take an international address. Please use the address listed below in the address fields (including the word "INTERNATIONAL") so that your application can be processed:
>
> INTERNATIONAL
> 4300 Biscayne Blvd. Suite 203
> Miami, Florida 33137
>
> Please also email me back with your full mailing address (including phone number) so that we can forward your induction kit there once you complete your steps. Thank you!

## Process: Induction Kits Processing & Shipment

**When this comes up:** A member asks about their kit delivery, shipment location, what's in the kit, when to expect it, or has a challenge with delivery.

**What is an induction kit:** A package sent to members after completing all steps of the Foundations of Leadership program and having those steps approved by chapter leadership. It includes a certificate of completion, lapel pin, car decal, and other induction items (varies by chapter). This is NOT a welcome package — it is only sent after all steps are completed and approved.

**Prerequisites:** Member must complete all steps of the Foundations of Leadership program (Orientation, Leadership Training Day, Speaker Broadcasts, Success Networking Teams) AND have all submissions reviewed and approved by chapter leadership before a kit is sent.

**How kits are sent — by chapter type:**

**Standard chapters (no Induction Kit Fee required):**
- Kit is sent to the chapter at the time of their scheduled induction ceremony
- If the member wants it shipped to their home address instead: they pay the **$10.00 Induction Kit Fee (IKF)**, processed through Authorize
- If a member wants their kit before the induction ceremony or needs a replacement: $10.00 IKF applies — see Pricing List

**Online chapters (always require IKF):**
- Kit is always mailed directly to the address in the member's profile — never to the chapter
- Online members who have not yet paid an IKF: MEX Specialist adds the IKF at **$0.00** and adds the transaction to the member's Feather account
- Kits are processed on the **1st and 15th of each month** (or closest business day); status updates in Feather to reflect the actual processing date

**Timelines:**
- Processing: up to **3 weeks** (longer during high-volume periods like end of semester)
- Shipping: **7–10 business days** after processing
- Carrier: typically USPS ground; FedEx or UPS may also be used

**HubSpot snippets:**
- `#Induction Steps - Email (DETAILED)`
- `#What's in a kit - Chat`
- `#Where's my kit / steps not done - Email`
- Check the tracking inbox for member induction kits

**Common questions:**

*What's in the induction kit?*
- Certificate of completion, car decal, lapel pin, and other induction items. Contents vary by chapter. Note: some chapters (e.g., SNHU) have modified kits.

*What does a member need to do to receive it?*
- Complete the full Foundations of Leadership program AND have all steps approved by chapter leadership. Kits cannot be sent before that under any circumstances.

*How long will it take?*
- Up to 3 weeks for processing, then 7–10 business days to ship. High-volume periods (end of semester, graduation season) may cause additional delays.

*Member wants kit before the induction ceremony / needs a replacement?*
- $10.00 IKF applies in both cases. Process through Authorize. See Pricing List.
