## Overview

All MEx Specialists handle scholarship inquiries — no need to transfer to the Awards Pipeline. Respond within 24 hours. Encourage members to apply for every scholarship they're eligible for.

**Work is assigned from the Awards Pipeline at:** 11 AM, 1 PM, 3 PM, and 5 PM

## Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Monitor & Self-Assign Tickets
- In HubSpot → Tickets → view tickets assigned to Kimberly → reassign to yourself when workload allows
- Do not self-assign tickets belonging to another agent unless directed by MEL
- Try to solve the problem before referring to the Scholars App

### Step 2: Confirm Key Details Before Responding
- Is this a general NSLS scholarship or chapter-specific?
- Is the member pre-inducted or inducted?
- What scholarship name or season are they asking about?
- Use the correct HubSpot Snippet or Email Template
- Attach the Scholarship PDF or direct to the Awards Overview Page

### Step 3: How to Find Scholarship Applications
1. Go to the Benefits Overview tab
2. Select the fourth box: **"Apply for Scholarship"**
3. Click the red **"Learn More"** button → Scholars App (scholarsapp.com)
4. Create an account if not already registered

**Scholars App issues — contact:**
- General: hello@scholarsapp.com
- Technical: support@scholarsapp.com
- Phone: (808) 374-4559

### Step 4: Respond & Encourage
- Warm, encouraging tone — especially for members who missed deadlines
- Encourage them to complete the application by the deadline
- Use: *"We're here to support you as you pursue your goals!"*
- Escalate complex inquiries; consider a phone call if needed
- Follow up within 24 hours if more research is needed

## FAQs

**Where are the scholarship criteria and guidelines?**
In the NSLS Scholarships & Awards Guide on the website. If the page asks for a nomination code, scroll past it to find the guide under "Helping You Reach Your Goals."

**Where are the applications?**
Through Scholars App at scholarsapp.com. For an overview, provide the scholarship PDF.

**Can members apply to scholarships from a chapter they're not in?**
Chapter-specific scholarships are usually limited to that chapter's members. All members can apply for national scholarships at nsls.org/scholarships.

**Did I win? How do I find out?**
Winner release dates are in the "Open Scholarship Periods and Deadlines" section of the Scholarship & Awards Guide. If the date has passed, check "Featured Scholarship Winners" on the NSLS website. Winners are also notified by email.

**Who should write my letter of recommendation?**
A professional, academic, or personal reference who knows the applicant and can speak to why they deserve the award. NSLS staff cannot write LORs for NSLS scholarships.

**Technical issues with Scholars App?**
Go to scholarsapp.com and try to resolve first. If unresolved: hello@scholarsapp.com / support@scholarsapp.com / (808) 374-4559.

**Can I put someone else's name on my W-9 or W-8 BEN?**
No — the applicant/recipient must enter their own details.
