# SOS Escalations to MEX Leadership SOP

**Written by:** Atrayu Polhemus  
**Last updated:** 6/28/2025  
**Applies to:** Any situation where a SOS Support Specialist needs to escalate to MEX Leadership (MEL)

---

## Purpose

This SOP guides SOS Support Specialists on when and how to escalate a member concern to MEX Leadership (MEL). Escalations are reserved for urgent issues that require manager intervention, cannot be resolved by the SOS, or fall outside standard policy.

---

## Definitions

- **MEL** — Member Experience Leadership (Team Leads, Director of Member Experience)
- **SOS** — Tenured Support agent who assists other MEX Specialists

---

## When to Escalate

Escalate to MEL when:
- De-escalation attempts have failed
- The issue requires manager approval (e.g., refund exceptions, graduation set refunds)
- The request falls outside standard policy
- There are safety concerns, repeat escalations, or third-party involvement
- The member explicitly requests a manager

---

## Process

### Step 1 — Attempt to De-Escalate First

Try to resolve the situation yourself using the CARE framework before escalating. Only proceed if you cannot resolve it independently.

### Step 2 — Tag a Team Lead in Slack

If de-escalation is unsuccessful:
- Post in **#sos-escalation** and tag a Team Lead
- Or send a **direct message** to the Team Lead with full details

> **If no Team Lead is available:** Escalate to the Director of Member Experience or other designated leader.

### Step 3 — Share Full Case Details

While the member is waiting, provide the on-duty manager with:
- Full context from the HubSpot ticket (policy cited, request details, member history)
- What was already attempted
- Answer all questions the manager has

### Step 4 — Transfer or Schedule Callback

**If transferring by phone:**
Confirm the manager is ready, then perform a **warm transfer**.

**If transferring by chat:**
Reply to the member:
> *"I am escalating this to a manager, and they will be joining the chat soon. Thank you for your patience, and I hope you have a wonderful day."*

**If MEL is not available:**
Inform the member that a manager will call back within **24 business hours**, then:
1. Team Lead adds a task in HubSpot
2. Set the Category to **"Manager Escalation"**
3. Manager logs a call and completes the task in HubSpot when they follow up

---

## Prerequisites

- HubSpot access: ability to review, update, assign tickets, log calls, create tasks, and use the "Manager Escalation" category
- Slack access: #sos-escalation and/or #mex-leadership channels
- 8x8 phone and chat: ability to perform warm transfers
- Familiarity with the CARE framework and de-escalation best practices
