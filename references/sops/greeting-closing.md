# Greeting & Closing SOP

**Written by:** Kimberly Campbell  
**Source:** MEX SOP — MEx Greeting & Closing (Whisper Prompts)

---

## CARE Philosophy

Every interaction should reflect **CARE**: **Connected, Attentive, Responsible, Enthusiastic.**

---

## Step 1 — Listen for the Whisper Prompt

Before answering, listen for the whisper prompt in your headset (only you hear it, not the member). It tells you which queue the call is coming from:
- **General Queue** → standard NSLS greeting
- **Specialty Queue** → school-specific greeting with white glove service

If you miss the whisper prompt, default to the General Queue greeting and confirm the member's school during the call.

---

## Step 2 — Greet the Member

**General Queue Greeting:**
> "Thank you for calling The National Society of Leadership and Success. This is [Your Name]. Who do I have the pleasure of speaking with today?"

**Specialty Queue Greeting:**
> "Thank you for calling the [School Name] Member Experience Line for The National Society of Leadership and Success. This is [Your Name]. Who do I have the pleasure of speaking with today?"

- Always say the full school name in Specialty Queue greetings.
- Tip: Smile when you speak — it comes through in your tone.

---

## Step 3 — Verify the Member

After they share their name:
> "Thank you, [Member Name], for calling today. I'm glad I received your call and I'll do all I can to help you. First, I just need to ask a few questions to protect your privacy and security."

Complete verification per the Call Verification SOP, then:
> "Thank you so much for helping me verify your account. What had you reach out to us today, and how can I help you?"

---

## Step 4 — Answer and Assist

- Listen attentively and take notes if multiple questions are asked
- Use CARE philosophy throughout the conversation
- Celebrate the member's progress if appropriate (nearing induction, completing a step, joining this term)

---

## Step 5 — Close the Call

**General Queue Closing:**
> "It's been great speaking with you today! We appreciate you as a member and I'm always happy to help. Is there anything else I can assist you with before we wrap up? Thank you for reaching out, and have a great day from all of us at The NSLS."

**Specialty Queue Closing:**
> "It's been wonderful connecting with you today! We appreciate you as a valued member of the [School Name] Chapter. Is there anything else I can help you with before we finish? Thank you for reaching out, and have a great day from the [School Name] Chapter and The NSLS."

- Always say the full school name in Specialty Queue closings.
- You may abbreviate "The National Society of Leadership and Success" to "The NSLS" in closings.

## FAQs

**Do I need to thank the caller every time?**  
Yes — always. It builds trust in the first 30 seconds per CARE philosophy.

**Can I adjust the greeting/closing to my style?**  
You can adjust tone and flow, but all required components must stay: thanks, your name, school/NSLS mention, offer of help, personalized closing.

**What if I'm not sure which queue the call came from?**  
Default to General Queue greeting. Confirm the member's school during the conversation.
