# A&E Reimbursement — Offer Met SOP

**Written by:** Kara Klimuszko | **Last updated:** 7/10/2025

## What This Covers

Processing refunds for Advanced & Executive (A&E) course purchases where the member completed the program within one year of purchase. This applies **only** to members who enrolled during the Fall 2024 testing period (4/26/2024–10/18/2024).

## Who Qualifies

- Member must have enrolled between **4/26/2024 and 10/18/2024** (Fall 2024 testing phase)
- Member must have **completed A&E courses within one year of purchase**
- If both Certificate and Credit Recommended were purchased, each can be refunded separately (or both if both completed on time)
- Not all members who joined during this period received the offer — always check their transactions to confirm

## Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Verify Eligibility in Feather
1. Check the member's Feather progress to confirm completion
2. Go to the [Refundable on Completion report in Feather](https://app.nsls.org/feather/sales/reports/refundable-on-completion)
3. Enter the member's email address and set "Meets Completion Criteria" to **Yes**
4. If member appears on report → proceed to Step 2
5. If member does NOT appear → they have not completed coursework. Verify progress again. If they have completed but aren't on the report → add to Help Desk tracker for a tech ticket

### Step 2: Begin the Refund
1. Select "View" to open the member's profile
2. Go to **Transactions** to process the refund
3. Enter **ENROLLMENT OFFER MET** as the reason — this is required

### Step 3: Process Based on Timing

| Timeframe | Action |
|---|---|
| Within 120 days of purchase | Process refund normally — return to original payment method. No Billing team needed. |
| Beyond 120 days (most cases) | Connect with Billing team and request an ACH refund. See [SOP: MEx ACH Refunds](https://docs.google.com/document/d/18DnIG918YnmT1xGPo7QPxXVockmvJ1a4Nd3sxtFc63g/edit) |

### Post-Procedure
Submit the ACH refund request to Billing on behalf of the member to close the loop.

## Key Rules

- Reason code must say **ENROLLMENT OFFER MET** exactly
- Most completions will be past 120 days — ACH is the standard path
- Refund applies per course individually (Certificate and/or Credit Recommended)
- Always verify transactions to confirm the member actually received the offer
