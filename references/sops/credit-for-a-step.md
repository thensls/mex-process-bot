# Credit for a Step SOP

**Written by:** MEX Team | **Category:** Feather / Enrollment

## What This Covers

How to handle requests where a member did not receive credit for a completed Foundations of Leadership (FOL) step.

**Steps in FOL:** Orientation, Leadership Training Day, Speaker Broadcasts, Success Networking Team (SNT) meetings

---

## Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Identify the Step Type

**For Orientation, Leadership Training Day, or SNT meetings:**
- Direct the member to contact their **Chapter Leaders** — chapter is responsible for reviewing and approving these steps
- If no response within **72 hours**, advise them to reach out again via the Chapter Information section in the Members Area
- If member already emailed and still no response after 72 hours → escalate to the chapter's **NOR (National Office Representative)**
- ⚠️ MEX should NOT email Chapter Leaders directly — always go through the NOR

**For Speaker Broadcasts → proceed to Step 2**

### Step 2: Confirm Which Broadcast

Ask the member which specific Speaker Broadcast they watched and did not receive credit for.

### Step 3: Verify They Actually Watched It

Before granting credit, confirm the member watched the broadcast by asking a friendly verification question:

> "We'd love to hear your thoughts! What was your favorite part or biggest takeaway from the Speaker Broadcast?"

**Acceptable response:** Specific, genuine detail about the content
> *"I really liked her advice on finding sponsors, not just mentors. I didn't know the difference before."*

**Unacceptable response:** Vague or generic
> *"Yes, I watched it. It was good."*

- If vague → ask for more detail before granting credit
- If member cannot describe the video after follow-up → do not award credit
- ⚠️ **Archived broadcasts do not count** toward FOL completion — no credit can be given for archived videos regardless of whether they were watched

### Step 4: Assign Credit in Feather

Once confirmed:
1. Search for the member in Feather
2. Go to the **Progress** tab
3. Click **"Assign Progress Completion"**
4. Under **Type**, select **"Broadcast"**
5. Choose the correct speaker from the dropdown
6. Click **"Add Action"** to apply credit

### Step 5: Confirm with the Member

Let them know credit has been added with a warm message:

> "Thanks for sharing your thoughts! I've gone ahead and added credit for this speaker broadcast to your account."

---

## Key Rules

| Rule | Detail |
|---|---|
| Speaker Broadcast requirement | Must watch at least **30 minutes** of **3 different** broadcasts to complete the step |
| Credit timing | Should apply automatically once 30-minute mark is reached — only intervene if it didn't |
| Archived broadcasts | Do **not** count toward FOL completion — no credit for archived videos |
| Orientation / LTD / SNT | Must go through Chapter Leaders → NOR, not MEX directly |
| Verification required | Must get a meaningful description before granting broadcast credit |
