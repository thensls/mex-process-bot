# Unsubscribe a Contact SOP

**Category:** General / Communications

## What This Covers

Removing a contact from NSLS communications in HubSpot and Feather. Applies to members and prospective members across any channel.

⚠️ **Unsubscribe ≠ Delete & Disable.** Unsubscribe = stop communications only. Delete & Disable = full account removal. Use the Data Removal SOP for the latter.

---

## Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Verify the Contact

| Channel | Verification |
|---|---|
| Email | Proceed directly to Step 2 |
| Chat | Use #verify snippet first, then proceed |
| Phone | Ask for full name and email where they received messages |

### Step 2: Unsubscribe in HubSpot

1. Find the contact's HubSpot page
2. Send **#unsub** snippet
3. Click the contact's name to open Contact Page Details
4. Click **Actions** (next to contact name) → **"Opt out of email"**

### Step 3: Update Feather (If They Have an Active Account)

1. Open their Feather profile → **Edit**
2. Scroll to **Communication**
3. Set all boxes to **gray** (gray = OFF, blue = ON)
4. Add a brief note in the notes field (e.g., *"Unsubscribe request via chat – verified."*)

### Step 4: Add to Feather Blocked List (Prospective Members Only)

Current members who just want to unsubscribe typically do NOT need to be added to the blocked list. Use this step for prospective members who never want to be contacted again.

1. Open Feather Blocked List → **"Add Email"**
2. Enter email, first name, last name, and chapter (chapter field is required — error if blank)
3. Click **Save**
4. Run a search to confirm they appear on the blocked list

---

## Key Rules

- Always opt out in **both** HubSpot and Feather to fully stop outreach
- Blocked list = primarily for prospective members, not current members with a simple unsubscribe request
- Unsubscribe request does not mean the member wants their account deleted — clarify if unsure
- If they DO want full account removal → use the **Data Removal Requests SOP**
