# Chapter Transfer SOP

**Written by:** Atrayu Polhemus | **Last updated:** 6/28/2025

## What This Covers

How to transfer an NSLS member to a different chapter in Feather. Always confirm the new chapter is active AND accepting members before processing.

## Valid Reasons to Transfer

1. **Member changed schools** — wants to continue NSLS at their new institution
2. **Chapter inactivity** — original chapter is inactive, blocking step approvals or kit shipment
3. **Certificate name** — member wants new school's name on their A&E certificates
4. **Scheduling flexibility** — needs more flexible timing or prefers fully virtual (Online Chapter)
   - Note: Before transferring for flexibility, encourage the member to contact their Chapter Leaders to request online accommodations first

## When NOT to Transfer

- Member is already inducted and not pursuing additional certifications — no transfer needed
- NSLS encourages members to stay with their original chapter when possible

---

## Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Confirm Chapter is Active & Accepting Members

**Do NOT transfer a member into a chapter that is not accepting members.**

1. Search for the chapter in Feather
2. Check the **Status** field — must be "Active" (inactive chapters cannot receive transfers)
3. Check the **Accepting Members** field — must show a green "Yes"
   - Status alone does not confirm the chapter is accepting transfers — check both fields

### Step 2: Transfer in Feather

1. Open the member's Feather profile
2. Select the **Edit** tab
3. Go to **School Information**
4. Click **"Transfer to other Chapter"**
5. In the pop-up, type the chapter name and select the correct school from the dropdown
6. Click **"Transfer Member"** (red button) to complete
7. Member will see the change in their dashboard immediately

⚠️ **Never select "National Office"** — that chapter is for employee use only.

## Special Cases

### IKF (Induction Kit Fee) — School Change Transfers
- If the original chapter covered the $5.99 IKF but the new chapter does not (e.g., SNHU, National Online Chapter), **the member must pay the $5.99 fee**

### SNHU Chapter Transfer — Non-Inducted Members
- Members can choose between a **t-shirt or cords** until they are inducted (regardless of original enrollment date)
- If they purchased a graduation set at enrollment, they receive the **standard graduation set** — cords instead of a tassel charm (NOT the SNHU enrollment graduation set items)

### Inactive Chapter — No Transfer Available
- Let the member know the chapter is not accepting members
- If the member is enrolled at that school, they can start a new chapter:
  - [Start a Chapter](https://www.nsls.org/start-a-chapter)
  - [NSLS Internships](https://www.nsls.org/nsls-internships)
- A National Office Representative will follow up if they submit interest

### Chapter Not Accepting New Members
- Member may stay in their current chapter or wait until the new chapter reopens

---

## Key Rules

- Always check **both** Status AND Accepting Members before transferring
- Reason code / context matters — document the transfer reason
- Online Chapter = fully self-paced, member completes steps on their own schedule
- Never use "National Office" as a transfer destination
