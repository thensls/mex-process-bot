# Grammarly Setup SOP

**Written by:** KK | **Last updated:** 6/17/2025

## What This Covers

Installing Grammarly browser extension for real-time grammar and spelling checks across emails, chats, and written communications.

**Requires:** NSLS Gmail account access

---

## Setup Steps

1. Go to: [https://www.grammarly.com/signup?afterSurvey=recommend](https://www.grammarly.com/signup?afterSurvey=recommend)
2. Click **"Get Grammarly, It's free"**
3. Select **"Sign Up with Google"** and choose your **NSLS Gmail account**
4. Under "Most of my writing is for" → select **Work**
5. Click **Continue**
6. Under "Tell us about your team" → select **"Skip Personalization"**

Done — Grammarly will run automatically in your browser as you type.
