# Employee Complaint SOP

**Written by:** KK | **Last updated:** 7/8/2025 | **Internal use only**

## What This Covers

When an MEX Specialist receives a complaint about a fellow NSLS employee through any communication channel. This protects employee privacy and ensures only the appropriate manager is informed.

---

## Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Email the Appropriate Manager

1. Copy the details from the related HubSpot ticket
2. Paste into a new email from your NSLS Gmail
3. Subject line: **"Employee Complaint"**
4. Use the [2025 NSLS Org Chart](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1P3OOdI4jlilo-d9N8vUiAVGbIN1tSEOEEJbDZkDgnxU/edit) to identify the correct manager for the employee being complained about
5. Send to that manager only
6. **CC:** Director of Member Experience + your Team Lead
7. Address the manager by name in the email body

### Step 2: Note It in HubSpot

1. Create a note on the HubSpot ticket documenting:
   - Details of the complaint
   - All actions taken
   - That details were shared with the Director of Member Experience and the appropriate manager (include manager's name)
2. Pin the note to the top of the ticket: **Actions → Pin**

---

## Key Rules

- Do **not** share complaint details with anyone other than the appropriate manager, Director of MEx, and your Team Lead
- Always use the org chart to identify the correct manager — do not assume
- Always CC the Director of Member Experience and your Team Lead on every employee complaint email
- The HubSpot note must be pinned so it's visible at the top of the ticket
