# FAQ: Handling Calls About Former Online Advisors

**Source:** MEx_FAQ_Handling Calls About Former OA Advisors  
**Applies to:** Any call or chat where a member or chapter leader asks about a former Online Advisor (OA) or the advising transition

---

## Context

On **3/16**, two emails went out to online chapter leaders explaining the advising transition. Former OA advisors are **no longer with the organization** — do not offer their contact info. All online advising is now handled by the MEx Advising Team at **advising@nsls.org**.

---

## Know Which Email They Received

**EMAIL 1 — General Online Chapters**
- Subject: *"NSLS Online Advising — Here's What's New"*
- Sent to: All online chapter advisors, student presidents, and VPs (excluding whale schools)
- From: clientservices@nsls.org
- Key message: Chapter is now supported by the full MEx Advising Team. Reach them at advising@nsls.org or the same advisor email as always.

**EMAIL 2 — Whale Schools Only**
- Subject: *"An Upgrade to Your Chapter Support — Here's What's New"*
- Sent to: Large strategic online chapter partners (formerly managed by Stacy)
- From: clientservices@nsls.org
- Key message: Chapter now has direct access to Kimberly Campbell (Director of MEx) at kimberlyjcampbell@nsls.org, backed by her full advising team.

> **If unsure whether caller is a whale school:** Check the Whale School list (ask MEL for access) or default to Email 1 language.

## Definitions

- **Whale School** — Large strategic online chapter. Gets Email 2 language and kimberlyjcampbell@nsls.org contact.
- **General Online Chapter** — All other online chapters. Gets Email 1 language and advising@nsls.org contact.

## Call Scripts

Use as a guide — make it your own, keep the key points.

### "I'd like to speak to [advisor name]."

> "I completely understand — [name] was great to work with. I do want to let you know that our advising team has recently transitioned and [name] is no longer with the organization. The great news is that your chapter is now supported by our entire Member Experience Advising Team, so you have a full team in your corner rather than one person. You should have received an email about this on Monday — the subject line was [Email 1: 'NSLS Online Advising — Here's What's New' / Email 2 for whale schools: 'An Upgrade to Your Chapter Support — Here's What's New']. I'm happy to help you today, or you can reach us anytime at advising@nsls.org."

### "Can I get [advisor's] contact information?"

> "I'm not able to provide personal contact information, but I want to make sure you're taken care of. Everything that was handled by your former advisor is now handled by our Member Experience team. You can reach us at advising@nsls.org and we'll get back to you within 24 to 48 hours. Is there something I can help you with right now?"

### "Why did my advisor change? Nobody told me."

> "We did send a communication to chapter leaders on Monday about this change — the subject line was [Email 1: 'NSLS Online Advising — Here's What's New' / Email 2 for whale schools: 'An Upgrade to Your Chapter Support — Here's What's New']. It may have landed in spam — worth checking. Your chapter advisement is now supported by our full Member Experience Advising Team. It's actually an upgrade in terms of availability and response time. I'm sorry if the timing felt abrupt. Is there something specific I can help you with today?"

### "I've been working with [advisor name] on my FOL steps. Will my progress be lost?"

> "Your progress is completely safe — everything is tracked in your member profile and nothing has been lost. Our team has full visibility into where you are in the program. I can pick up right where you left off. Can you tell me which step you're currently working on?"

### "I emailed my advisor and never heard back."

> "I apologize for any gap in communication during this transition. Our team is now handling all online chapter advising and we want to make sure you get the support you need. Can you tell me what you reached out about? I'm happy to help you right now, or if it's more involved I'll make sure the right person follows up with you today."

### General advising question (FOL steps, program, membership)

Handle it — this is exactly what the advising team is here for. Use MEx Advising Snippets as your guide. If on a live call and unsure:
> "Great question — let me make sure I get you the right information. Can I follow up with you via email at advising@nsls.org within 24 hours?"

- **Never give out a former advisor's personal contact info**
- **advising@nsls.org** is the new home for all online advising support
- Response time commitment: **24–48 hours**
- MEx Leadership/Workforce took over the Advising pipeline on **3/17**
- Online System Submission Approval System (SAS) oversight began **3/18/2026**
