# Ignite MEx Support Plan SOP

**Category:** General | **Phase:** Beta launch (April 15, 2025)

## What This Covers

Supporting members during the beta rollout of Ignite — NSLS's new AI-powered career readiness product. MEx is support only, not training or tech.

**Key contact:** Kevin Prentiss, Head of Product — kprentiss@nsls.org  
**Beta access:** National Online Chapter members only (rolling invitations, ~100 every few days)

---

## Core Rules for All Interactions

- Only answer questions covered in this SOP — do NOT improvise or speculate
- **Copy Kevin (kprentiss@nsls.org) on every written response** (email, chat, text summary)
- After every phone call, send a summary email to the member and copy Kevin
- Log every interaction in HubSpot: category = **"Ignite"**, naming format = **"First Last - Ignite - Short Summary"**
- Log in the **MEx Exception Tracker – Ignite Tab** immediately

---

| Channel | If you can answer | If you can't answer |

## Questions MEx Can Answer

**What is Ignite?**
NSLS's new career readiness product — AI-powered, personalized experience. Currently includes the Clarity Track: 6 guided steps to explore strengths, values, and ideal career paths.

**Is there a cost?**
No additional cost. Included with NSLS membership.

**How do I log in?**
Use the same email as your NSLS membership. Members receive a magic link or can use "Sign in with Google" if Gmail-registered. If wrong email entered, an error appears — use exact NSLS login email.

**What is the Clarity Track?**
Six guided steps: (1) Discover strengths, (2) Identify inspirations, (3) Understand ideal work environment, (4) Choose career direction, (5) Perfect professional story, (6) Map journey to dream job.

**Who has access right now?**
Beta only — currently limited to Online Chapter members. Invitations rolling out in small waves.

---

## Questions That Must Go to Kevin — Do NOT Attempt to Answer

- Still can't log in using correct email
- A Clarity Track step is confusing or doesn't make sense
- Can they retake a section or change answers?
- Can they skip steps or customize the experience?
- Why was I invited but my friend wasn't?
- Feedback or suggestions about Ignite
- Will this replace Foundations of Leadership?
- Any question not in this SOP

→ Send summary email to member, copy Kevin, ask their preferred follow-up method (call/text/email)

---

## Key Rules

- Do not guess or troubleshoot Ignite issues beyond what's in this SOP
- Every single interaction must have: HubSpot ticket + Exception Tracker entry + summary email to member copying Kevin
- This process is temporary for the beta phase
