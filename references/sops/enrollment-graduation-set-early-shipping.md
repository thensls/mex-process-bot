# Enrollment Graduation Set — Early Shipping SOP

**Category:** Shop / Induction Kits

## What This Covers

Processing early shipping requests for members who purchased the Enrollment Graduation Set but have not yet completed all steps to induction. SOS approval is NOT required.

## Eligibility

- Member must have paid for the Graduation Set during enrollment
- Early shipping is allowed even if induction steps are not complete
- Standard shipping is free; expedited is available at member's cost (not required)
- Specialists should reinforce completing steps first, but may fulfill if member directly requests or escalates

---

## Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Confirm Shipping Method & Charge If Needed

1. Ask if standard ground (7–10 business days) works, or if they need it faster
2. **Standard shipping** → no additional charge, proceed
3. **Expedited shipping** → get estimated cost from the Shop (add their address at checkout), then charge via Authorize.net
4. Let member know expected delivery window based on shipping method and cutoff times (see below)
5. ⚠️ NSLS is not responsible for guaranteeing delivery by a specific date — the shipping carrier is

### Step 2: Sync the Order in Feather

1. Sync the Graduation Set order using the **Grad Set Dashboard** in Feather
2. Double-check the member's mailing address for accuracy
3. If personalized medallion is included, submit before **9:30 a.m. ET** for same-day processing

### Step 3: Add Comment on Shopify Order

1. Go to the **Shop Order #** (NOT the shop contact page)
2. Add a comment tagging **@Rebecca Hebert** and **@Shop Team**
3. Include: shipping method, amount charged, any delivery deadline
4. No greetings or pleasantries in Shop order comments

**Example:** `@Rebecca Hebert @Shop Team 3-5 day Shipping, paid $15.75 - Needs it by May 10`

---

## Shipping Options & Cutoff Times

| Method | Estimated Delivery | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| Standard Ground | 7–10 business days | Free |
| 3–5 Day Shipping | 3–5 business days | Varies by location |
| 2-Day Rush | 2 business days | Varies by location |

**To get the correct shipping cost:** Go to the NSLS Shop checkout, enter member's address, note the estimated amount, then collect payment via Authorize.net.

### Processing Cutoffs (Mon–Fri only)

| Order Type | Same-Day Cutoff |
|---|---|
| Non-personalized | 12:00 p.m. EST (carriers pick up 3:00–3:30 p.m.) |
| Personalized (engraved medallion) | 9:30 a.m. EST |

- Orders after cutoff → ship **next business day**
- Expedited on personalized items: in by 9:30 a.m. = ships same day expedited; after 9:30 a.m. = ships next business day expedited
- Ground shipments have **no guaranteed delivery date**
